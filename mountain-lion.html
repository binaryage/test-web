Mountain Lion status

Good news! The latest release of TotalFinder fully works under Mountain Lion (OSX 10.8).

I'm committed to bring all TotalFinder features to Mountain Lion.
Also I will continue to support Lion in the foreseeable future. I have dropped support for Snow Leopard.
