Free licenses for users

I will grant a free license to everyone who meets any of these criteria:

  1. helped directly in TotalFinder development (e.g. translators -> visit this page to help with translation)
  2. created or significantly contributed to an open-source project I've used in TotalFinder
  3. is a public speaker and sends me a link to his/her talk (must be recorded and publicly available on youtube or similar site)
  4. is a Google engineer with a valid someone@google.com email address
    - this is my "thanks" for the Chrome project and great meals at GDD
  5. is an Apple employee with a valid someone@apple.com email address
    - this is my general "thank you" for making awesome products
  6. has bitcoins and wants to trade them for TotalFinder - also I offer bitcoin earnings in my affiliate program
  7. feels that he/she should get a free license for some other reason and convinces me

Please drop me an email and I will send you a free license link if you meet the criteria.
